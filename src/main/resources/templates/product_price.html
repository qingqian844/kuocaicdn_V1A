Metered Pricing

The open source edition keeps only balance-based metered traffic billing.

Billing Rules

  • Traffic is billed by actual usage in GB.
  • Generated traffic orders use internal balance payment only.
  • Administrators can manually recharge or deduct user balances.
  • Online payment, withdrawal, proxy commission, and traffic package purchases are not included.
  • 1 PB = 1024 TB, 1 TB = 1024 GB, 1 GB = 1024 MB.
